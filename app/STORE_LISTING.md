## Full Description

Your personal snooker training companion — built by players, for players.

CueUp helps you structure your practice, track your progress, and build the habits that turn casual players into serious competitors. Whether you're working on your break-building, sharpening your safety game, or just trying to pot more consistently, CueUp gives you the tools to practise with purpose.

▸ STRUCTURED TRAINING SESSIONS
Start a session from scratch or pick from ready-made routines — 30-min Potting Focus, Break-Building Session, Safety & Position Mix, or a Quick Warm-Up. CueUp comes loaded with 21 expertly designed exercises across 7 categories: potting, safety, break building, positional play, cueing fundamentals, warm-ups, and match practice. Each drill includes setup diagrams, clear instructions, best-practice tips, and a suggested duration. Log your results as you go, add exercises mid-session, and pick up where you left off if you need to pause.

▸ BREAK LOGGING
Record every break — from practice, matches, or league play. CueUp automatically tracks your personal bests across each context and shows your highest breaks at a glance. See exactly when and where you hit your milestones, with a visual trend chart of your recent scores.

▸ MATCH TRACKER
Log your competitive matches with opponent names, frame-by-frame scores, high breaks per frame, and results. Separate friendly and league matches, track your win rate, and review full frame-by-frame breakdowns after each match to see how your practice is paying off on the table.

▸ PROGRESS DASHBOARD
See your improvement over time with personal bests, rolling averages, break trends, practice streaks, and weekly session counts. CueUp analyses your training to highlight neglected categories and weak exercises — so you always know where to focus next.

▸ TIPS & GUIDANCE
Browse categorised tips covering fundamentals, cueing technique, safety mindset, mental game, and practice habits. Quick reminders you can read between frames or before a session.

▸ BUILT FOR SNOOKER PLAYERS
— 21 built-in drills with 6 scoring types (score out of, count potted, break value, pass/fail, timed, notes only)
— Difficulty levels from beginner to advanced
— Pre-built session routines to get practising in seconds
— Session mood tracking to connect your mental state to your performance
— Weekly session goals to keep you accountable
— Intelligent suggestions for your next session based on neglected areas
— Set your player name and personalise your experience
— Clean, snooker-green interface with automatic light and dark mode
— Works entirely offline — all your data stays on your device

▸ YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE
CueUp stores everything locally on your phone. No account required, no data sent to servers, no ads. Just you and your game.
